My new journey as a scrimba student

Being a Scrimba student is a rewarding and engaging experience that combines the flexibility of online learning with the interactivity of a traditional classroom setting. As a Scrimba student, you'll have access to high-quality video lectures, live coding sessions, and interactive coding challenges that will help you master the skills you need to become a proficient developer. You'll also have the opportunity to connect with other students from around the world, share your work, and receive feedback from experienced instructors. Whether you're a beginner just starting out or an experienced developer looking to upskill, Scrimba offers a supportive and dynamic learning community that will help you achieve your goals.
