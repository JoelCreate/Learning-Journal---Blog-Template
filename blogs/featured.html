My new journey as a scrimba student

Being a Scrimba student is a rewarding and engaging experience that combines the flexibility of online learning with the interactivity of a traditional classroom setting. As a Scrimba student, you'll have access to high-quality video lectures, live coding sessions, and interactive coding challenges that will help you master the skills you need to become a proficient developer. You'll also have the opportunity to connect with other students from around the world, share your work, and receive feedback from experienced instructors. Whether you're a beginner just starting out or an experienced developer looking to upskill, Scrimba offers a supportive and dynamic learning community that will help you achieve your goals.

Coding finally started to make sense!

As a new Scrimba student, I am excited to embark on this journey of learning and discovery. I have always been fascinated by technology and the way it can be used to solve complex problems and improve people's lives. And now, I am thrilled to be taking the first steps towards becoming a proficient web developer.

One of the things that drew me to Scrimba was the interactive nature of the platform. With its innovative "learn by doing" approach, I know that I'll be able to develop my skills in a practical and engaging way. Whether it's watching screencasts, working on coding challenges, or getting feedback from instructors and fellow students, I am eager to dive in and start learning.

Overall, I can't wait to see what this new journey as a Scrimba student will bring. I'm excited to learn new skills, tackle challenging projects, and ultimately, work towards becoming a skilled web developer. I know it won't be easy, but with the support of Scrimba's platform and community, I'm ready to take on this challenge and see where it takes me.
